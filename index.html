how to prevent from virus

wash your place

Regularly clean and disinfect frequently touched surfaces such as doorknobs, countertops, and electronics to reduce the risk of virus transmission.

maintain distance

Keep a safe distance (around 6 feet) from people, especially those who are coughing, sneezing, or feeling unwell, to minimize the chance of catching a virus through droplets.

dont touch face

Avoid touching your face, particularly your eyes, nose, and mouth, as this can easily transfer viruses from your hands into your body.
